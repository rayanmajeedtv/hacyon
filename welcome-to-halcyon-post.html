Welcome to Halcyon Post

The warmest of welcomes to Halcyon Post! We are Jay Mac, Tim Symes and Jen Randall – three film editors and directors turned friends who love to build stories here in sunny/soggy/glorious Squamish BC. 

We teamed up to create a company that offers narrative clarity, cinematic quality and creative soul to every project we dive into, and lets us enjoy life outside of the edit cave too. We love what we do and are very proud to be sharing it with you here on our beautiful website designed by the one and only Jonathan Creative with branding by local Stay Outside.
